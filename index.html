About Me

I am a third year undergraduate student at Rice University pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Statistics. I am interested in optimization, whether its enhancing the user-experience or using data to drive decisions. I am passionate about women in STEM, ultimate frisbee, and good coffee.
I came into Rice University as a Chemical Engineer major. I was amazed by how chemical engineers use math, physics, and chemistry to optimize and enhance the world around us. After taking my first CS class, I realized optimization is an integral part of CS and I fell in love with the challenge that CS brings. Now, instead of using physics and chemistry, I use technology and math to change the world and make an impact.
